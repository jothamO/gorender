# Code of Conduct (Draft)

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive project community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas with evidence.
- Give actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Disruptive or hostile conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, edit, lock, or remove content and restrict participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report concerns through the security/support contact path defined in `SECURITY.md` and `SUPPORT.md`.
